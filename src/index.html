Justice Has Entered the Digital Age

Curia is a cutting-edge enterprise judicial application that automates the end-to-end process of the justice system, empowering both governments, courts and institutions to deliver smarter, faster, and more accountable justice.

See Curia in action

More than a software, but a revolution in the justice system.

Curia is the innovative solution Nigeria’s judiciary has been waiting for. Unlike traditional judicial software, Curia integrates legal counsel, law enforcement, prosecutors, courts, correctional and remand centers into a unified digital platform.

Built from the ground up to serve the people who move justice forward, judges, magistrates, lawyers, registrars, commissioners for oaths, bailiffs, prosecutors and litigants. Curia empowers every player in the justice system with the tools to work faster, smarter, and more securely.

Curia is a product of Union Systems in collaboration with legal experts to drive judicial transformation that's long overdue. From faster filings to real-time updates, virtual  hearings, speech-to-text transformation , digital affidavits and  paperless workflows, Curia is not the future, it’s the now.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Curia?

Curia is a digital justice platform designed to modernize and transform justice delivery across Nigeria. It enables the digitisation of court records, case management, and legal workflows for greater efficiency and transparency.

Who can use Curia

Curia is accessible to judiciary staff (judges, registrars, bailiffs), legal practitioners (lawyers, prosecutors), and authorized government agencies involved in justice administration.

What services does Curia provide?

Curia offers: Centralised digital case filing and retrieval, Court document indexing and archiving,
Real-time case tracking, Seamless communication between legal entities, Secure digital workflows.

How does Curia improve Nigeria’s justice system?

Curia reduces delays, eliminates paper-based inefficiencies, and ensures timely access to legal information—making the system faster, more transparent, and people-centered.

What devices support Curia?

Curia is web-based and mobile-friendly. It works on desktops, laptops, tablets, and secure mobile devices.

Can members of the public access Curia?

Yes. Curia is accessible to all stakeholders, which include members of the public.
